AI-Powered Customer Subscription Analysis

Welcome!

This AI agent allows users to ask questions about customer subscription data and receive data-driven insights on customer activity, plan distribution, category performance, and signup trends. It is designed to support business analysis by transforming structured subscription data into clear, actionable insights.

Dataset Columns

  • Customer_ID – Unique identifier for each customer
  • Subscription_Category – Type of service subscribed to (e.g., Music, Sports, Gaming)
  • Subscription_Plan – Pricing tier selected (Basic, Standard, Premium)
  • Monthly_Price – Monthly cost of the subscription
  • Signup_Date – Date the customer started the subscription
  • Status – Current subscription status (Active or Churned)

AI Bot Capabilities

This AI agent is designed to answer the following business-focused questions based on the subscription dataset:

  • How many total customers are there?
  • How many customers are currently active?
  • How many customers have churned?
  • How many customers are subscribed to each subscription plan (Basic, Standard, Premium)?
  • Which subscription plan has the highest number of active customers?
  • How many customers are subscribed to each subscription category?
  • Which subscription category has the highest number of churned customers?
  • How does the average monthly subscription price vary by month across all three years?
  • Which subscription plan generates the highest estimated monthly revenue?
  • How many customers signed up in each year?
